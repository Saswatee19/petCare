Our Services

Our Services

From nose to tail — we cover every aspect of your pet's wellbeing under one roof.

Wellness Check Ups

Annual and bi-annual physicals to keep your pet in peak condition,with detailed health reports and personalised care plans.

Vaccination & Prevention

Tailored vaccination schedules, parasite prevention and flea & tick treatments to protect your pet year-round.

Surgery & Recovery

Spay/neuter, orthopaedic repair, soft-tissue procedures and post-operative care in our sterile surgical suite.

Diagnostics & Imaging

In-house lab tests, digital X-rays, ultrasound and ECG for fast,accurate diagnosis with no referrals needed.

Dental Care

Professional dental cleaning, tooth extraction and oral health assessments for a healthier pet.

Emergency & Critical Care

Round-the-clock emergency services with a dedicated ICU for critically ill patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pet care services.

Since 2006, PawCare Veterinary Hospital has been Bhubaneswar’s trusted partner in pet wellness. For over a decade, we have been dedicated to delivering compassionate, expert care for every stage of your pet's life.

We offer a comprehensive range of services to keep your furry family members healthy. This includes routine wellness check-ups, vaccinations, specialized surgery, advanced diagnostics, and emergency care.

Our team consists of over 50 expert doctors who are highly trained in veterinary medicine. We believe that every tail-wag and purr matters, and our staff treats your beloved companions exactly how you would—with infinite patience, love, and professional expertise.

We operate 10+ state-of-the-art care centers across the region. Over the years, our experienced team has successfully treated more than 1,500 pets.

At PawCare, we aren't just treating pets; we are nurturing the joyful bond between you and your furry family members. Our combination of state-of-the-art facilities, extensive experience, and genuine compassion makes us Bhubaneswar’s premier choice for pet care.
