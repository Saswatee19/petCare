About Us

Since 2006,PawCare Veterinary Hospital has been Bhubaneswar’s trusted partner in pet wellness, dedicated to delivering compassionate, expert care for every stage of your pet's life. We believe that every tail-wag and purr matters, which is why our team of over 50 expert doctors treats your beloved companions exactly how you would—with infinite patience, love, and professional expertise. Having successfully treated more than 1,500 pets across our 10+ state-of-the-art care centers, we offer a comprehensive range of services from routine wellness check-ups and vaccinations to specialized surgery, diagnostics, and emergency care. At PawCare, we aren't just treating pets; we are nurturing the joyful bond between you and your furry family members.
